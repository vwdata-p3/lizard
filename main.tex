\begin{abstract}
This note specifies
``lizard''
a reversible injection from $\{0,\dotsc,255\}^{16}$
to ristretto255, a prime order elliptic curve group built 
    on top of Curve25519.
\end{abstract}

Curve25519, originally intended\cite{x25519} for 
Diffie--Hellman key exchange,
is now also commonly used for
digital signatures\cite{ed25519}
and public key encryption\cite{rfc6637},
and will likely find more popular applications
in the years to come.
One of the intricacies of designing
a cryptographical system
based on the Curve25519 group~$\G$
is the fact
that~$\G$
is almost ---but not precisely--- of prime order,
being isomorphic to~$\Z/\ell\Z \oplus \Z/8\Z$,
where~$\ell = 2^{252}+27742317777372353535851937790883648493$ is prime.
